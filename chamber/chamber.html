About Us

The Kansas City Chamber of Commerce is a leading organization dedicated to promoting economic growth and development in the Kansas City metropolitan area. Founded in 1887, we serve as a catalyst for business success, providing advocacy, networking, and resources to our members.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I become a member?

A: Becoming a member of the Kansas City Chamber of Commerce is easy. Simply visit our website and fill out the membership application form. Once approved, you'll gain access to a wide range of benefits and resources.

Q: What services does the Chamber offer?

A: The Chamber offers a variety of services to support businesses of all sizes. These include advocacy, networking events, educational workshops, and marketing opportunities. We also provide access to business resources, such as research reports and professional development programs.
